About Reemak Industries

Founded in 2021, Reemak Industries is an enlisted supplier with the Directorate General of Defense Purchase (DGDP) under the Ministry of Defense, Bangladesh. We specialize in reliably supplying military-grade equipment and essential services to the armed forces and affiliated institutions.
